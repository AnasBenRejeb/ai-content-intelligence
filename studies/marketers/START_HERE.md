## Essential Resources for Marketing Excellence

### Phase 1: Digital Marketing Fundamentals (Week 1-2)

#### Core Skills
1. **[Google Digital Garage](https://learndigital.withgoogle.com/digitalgarage)**
   - Digital marketing basics
   - SEO fundamentals
   - **Duration**: 40 hours
   - **Cost**: Free + Certification

2. **[HubSpot Academy](https://academy.hubspot.com/)**
   - Inbound marketing
   - Content marketing
   - Email marketing
   - **Duration**: 20 hours
   - **Cost**: Free + Certifications

3. **[Moz SEO Learning Center](https://moz.com/learn/seo)**
   - On-page SEO
   - Link building
   - Technical SEO
   - **Duration**: 15 hours

### Phase 2: Content Marketing (Week 3-4)

#### Key Resources
1. **[Content Marketing Institute](https://contentmarketinginstitute.com/)**
   - Content strategy
   - Distribution channels
   - **Duration**: 10 hours

2. **[Copyblogger](https://copyblogger.com/)**
   - Copywriting techniques
   - Conversion optimization
   - **Duration**: 8 hours

3. **[Neil Patel Blog](https://neilpatel.com/blog/)**
   - Growth hacking
   - SEO strategies
   - **Duration**: 6 hours/week
