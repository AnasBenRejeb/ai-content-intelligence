## Essential Resources for AI Agent Development

### Phase 1: Foundation (Week 1-2)

#### Core ML/AI
1. **[Fast.ai - Practical Deep Learning](https://course.fast.ai/)**
   - Neural networks
   - Transfer learning
   - **Duration**: 40 hours
   - **Cost**: Free

2. **[DeepLearning.AI Specialization](https://www.deeplearning.ai/)**
   - Deep learning fundamentals
   - CNNs, RNNs, Transformers
   - **Duration**: 60 hours
   - **Cost**: $49/month

3. **[Stanford CS229 - Machine Learning](http://cs229.stanford.edu/)**
   - ML algorithms
   - Theory and practice
   - **Duration**: 50 hours
   - **Cost**: Free

### Phase 2: NLP & LLMs (Week 3-4)

#### Natural Language Processing
1. **[Hugging Face NLP Course](https://huggingface.co/course)**
   - Transformers
   - Fine-tuning
   - Deployment
   - **Duration**: 20 hours
   - **Cost**: Free

2. **[Stanford CS224N - NLP](http://web.stanford.edu/class/cs224n/)**
   - Word embeddings
   - Attention mechanisms
   - **Duration**: 40 hours
   - **Cost**: Free

3. **[LangChain Documentation](https://python.langchain.com/)**
   - Agent frameworks
   - Chain composition
   - **Duration**: 15 hours
   - **Cost**: Free

### Phase 3: Agent Systems (Week 5-6)

#### Multi-Agent Systems
1. **[Berkeley CS188 - AI](https://inst.eecs.berkeley.edu/~cs188/)**
   - Search algorithms
   - Game theory
   - Reinforcement learning
   - **Duration**: 50 hours

2. **[OpenAI Spinning Up in Deep RL](https://spinningup.openai.com/)**
   - RL fundamentals
   - Policy gradients
   - **Duration**: 30 hours

3. **[Multi-Agent Systems - Wooldridge](https://www.amazon.com/Introduction-MultiAgent-Systems-Michael-Wooldridge/dp/0470519460)**
   - Agent architectures
   - Coordination
   - **Duration**: 40 hours
