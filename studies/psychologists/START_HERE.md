## Essential Resources for Content Psychology

### Phase 1: Cognitive Psychology (Week 1-2)

#### Core Concepts
1. **[Cognitive Load Theory](https://www.nngroup.com/articles/minimize-cognitive-load/)**
   - Working memory limitations
   - Chunking information
   - **Duration**: 2 hours

2. **[Thinking, Fast and Slow - Daniel Kahneman](https://www.amazon.com/Thinking-Fast-Slow-Daniel-Kahneman/dp/0374533555)**
   - System 1 vs System 2 thinking
   - Cognitive biases
   - **Duration**: 20 hours

3. **[The Psychology of Persuasion - Robert Cialdini](https://www.influenceatwork.com/)**
   - 6 principles of influence
   - Social proof, scarcity, authority
   - **Duration**: 15 hours

### Phase 2: Behavioral Psychology (Week 3-4)

#### Key Resources
1. **[Hooked - Nir Eyal](https://www.nirandfar.com/hooked/)**
   - Hook model
   - Habit formation
   - **Duration**: 8 hours

2. **[BJ Fogg's Behavior Model](https://behaviormodel.org/)**
   - Motivation, ability, triggers
   - Behavior design
   - **Duration**: 4 hours

3. **[Nudge - Thaler & Sunstein](https://www.amazon.com/Nudge-Improving-Decisions-Health-Happiness/dp/014311526X)**
   - Choice architecture
   - Behavioral economics
   - **Duration**: 12 hours

### Phase 3: Content Psychology (Week 5-6)

#### Practical Applications
1. **[Nielsen Norman Group - UX Research](https://www.nngroup.com/articles/)**
   - Eye-tracking studies
   - Reading patterns
   - **Duration**: 10 hours

2. **[BuzzSumo - Content Research](https://buzzsumo.com/resources/)**
   - Viral content analysis
   - Emotional triggers
   - **Duration**: 6 hours

3. **[Copyblogger - Copywriting Psychology](https://copyblogger.com/)**
   - Headline formulas
   - Emotional copywriting
   - **Duration**: 8 hours

### Research Papers

1. **"The Psychology of Virality"** - Jonah Berger
   - URL: https://jonahberger.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/ViralityB.pdf
   - Why: Understanding what makes content spread

2. **"Emotional Contagion in Social Networks"**
   - URL: https://www.pnas.org/content/111/24/8788
   - Why: How emotions spread through text

3. **"The Power of Storytelling"**
   - URL: https://hbr.org/2014/03/why-your-brain-loves-good-storytelling
   - Why: Narrative psychology
