## Essential Resources for Leadership

### Phase 1: AI & Technology Strategy (Week 1-2)

#### Understanding AI
1. **[AI For Everyone - Andrew Ng](https://www.coursera.org/learn/ai-for-everyone)**
   - AI fundamentals
   - Business applications
   - **Duration**: 10 hours
   - **Cost**: Free

2. **[Harvard Business Review - AI Articles](https://hbr.org/topic/artificial-intelligence)**
   - AI strategy
   - Implementation
   - **Duration**: 8 hours

3. **[MIT Sloan - AI in Business](https://mitsloan.mit.edu/ideas-made-to-matter/artificial-intelligence-business)**
   - Case studies
   - Best practices
   - **Duration**: 6 hours

### Phase 2: Business Strategy (Week 3-4)

#### Strategic Thinking
1. **[Good Strategy Bad Strategy - Richard Rumelt](https://www.amazon.com/Good-Strategy-Bad-Difference-Matters/dp/0307886239)**
   - Strategy fundamentals
   - Competitive advantage
   - **Duration**: 15 hours

2. **[Blue Ocean Strategy](https://www.blueoceanstrategy.com/)**
   - Market creation
   - Value innovation
   - **Duration**: 12 hours

3. **[Playing to Win - A.G. Lafley](https://www.amazon.com/Playing-Win-Strategy-Really-Works/dp/142218739X)**
   - Strategic choices
   - Execution
   - **Duration**: 10 hours

### Phase 3: Leadership & Management (Week 5-6)

#### Leadership Excellence
1. **[High Output Management - Andy Grove](https://www.amazon.com/High-Output-Management-Andrew-Grove/dp/0679762884)**
   - Management principles
   - Team building
   - **Duration**: 12 hours

2. **[The Hard Thing About Hard Things - Ben Horowitz](https://www.amazon.com/Hard-Thing-About-Things-Building/dp/0062273205)**
   - Tough decisions
   - Crisis management
   - **Duration**: 10 hours

3. **[Measure What Matters - John Doerr](https://www.amazon.com/Measure-What-Matters-Google-Foundation/dp/0525536221)**
   - OKRs framework
   - Goal setting
   - **Duration**: 8 hours
