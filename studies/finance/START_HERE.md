### Key Metrics Framework

```
Revenue Metrics:
- MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue)
- ARR (Annual Recurring Revenue)
- ARPU (Average Revenue Per User)
- Revenue Growth Rate

Customer Metrics:
- CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)
- LTV (Lifetime Value)
- LTV:CAC Ratio
- Payback Period

Efficiency Metrics:
- Gross Margin
- EBITDA Margin
- Rule of 40
- Magic Number

Retention Metrics:
- Churn Rate
- NRR (Net Revenue Retention)
- GRR (Gross Revenue Retention)
- Cohort Retention
```
